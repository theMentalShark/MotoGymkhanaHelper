WHAT THIS MEASURES

Pure ARCore tracking, no lighthouse, no laser. You anchor the course in AR, walk it out placing real cones on the ghost markers, then walk back and re-check each one.

The re-check distance is the accumulated drift. It also logs how far you walked, so you get drift as a % of path length.

PROCEDURE — single-cone sighting

Place ONE cone where you want the course to start. No ground-plane guess, no second measured point — the origin's true 3D spot is triangulated from a few camera sightings.

  1. Aim the centre cross at the base of that origin cone, tap ADD SIGHT. Step around it ~a metre or two and repeat 2–3× from different angles.
  2. Tap SET ORIGIN — the rays intersect at the cone's real position + ground height.
  3. Rotate the course (+/−) to aim it over the lot, then LOCK COURSE.
  4. Check overlay from a few angles; if off, tap ↻ Re-sight and redo.
  5. Hide UI (top-right) any time the panels block your view.
  6. Aim the ring at each real cone base and hold steady — CHECK unlocks once the reading settles (~0.5s within 2cm), logging its position vs. the ghost.
