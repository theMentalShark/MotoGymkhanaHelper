WHAT THIS MEASURES

Pure ARCore tracking, no lighthouse, no laser. You anchor the course in AR, walk it out placing real cones on the ghost markers, then walk back and re-check each one.

The re-check distance is the accumulated drift. It also logs how far you walked, so you get drift as a % of path length.

PROCEDURE

  1. Point at the ground, move phone slowly until the ring locks on.
  2. Tap to drop the course origin (this is the SW corner).
  3. Rotate with the slider so the course fits your lot. Lock it.
  4. Walk to each ghost cone, put a real cone on it, tap PLACED.
  5. When all are down, walk back around. Aim the ring at each real cone base and tap CHECK.
  6. Read the drift table. Verify a couple with a tape measure.
