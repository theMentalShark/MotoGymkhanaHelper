WHAT THIS MEASURES

Pure ARCore tracking, no lighthouse, no laser. You anchor the course in AR, walk it out placing real cones on the ghost markers, then walk back and re-check each one.

The re-check distance is the accumulated drift. It also logs how far you walked, so you get drift as a % of path length.

PROCEDURE

  1. Point the phone at the ground — the course follows wherever the camera's center beam hits, live.
  2. Use +/− to rotate the course (pivots around the green start/finish box) and fine-tune ground height. Watch the cross-hatch ground overlay to verify height.
  3. Tap LOCK COURSE once it's positioned correctly.
  4. Walk the course placing real cones on the red ghost markers as you go — no separate placing step.
  5. Aim the ring at each real cone base and tap CHECK to log drift vs. the ghost target.
  6. Read the drift table. Verify a couple with a tape measure.
